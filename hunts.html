First-Time Tanzania Buffalo Safari

7–9 Day Classic African Hunt

Overview

Experience a true East African safari in the wild Selous ecosystem—one of the largest and most untouched hunting areas in Africa. This hunt is designed for hunters looking to pursue Cape buffalo alongside classic plains game in a traditional, fully guided setting.

Included

  • 7–9 days of guided hunting
  • 1–2 Cape buffalo (depending on hunt length)
  • Additional plains game opportunities (impala, wildebeest, zebra, warthog, hartebeest)
  • Professional hunter (PH), trackers, and hunting vehicles
  • Luxury tented safari camp accommodations
  • All meals, drinks, and camp services
  • Government hunting permits and conservation fees
  • Trophy field prep, dip & pack, and export handling

Base Cost

Most clients spend $25,000–$35,000 in total, depending on travel, trophy choices, and additional services.
