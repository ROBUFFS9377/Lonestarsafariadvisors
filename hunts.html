First-Time Tanzania Buffalo Safari

7–9 Day Classic African Hunt

Overview

Experience a true East African safari in the wild Selous ecosystem—one of the largest and most untouched hunting areas in Africa. This hunt is designed for hunters looking to pursue Cape buffalo alongside classic plains game in a traditional, fully guided setting.

What’s Included

  • 7–9 days of guided hunting
  • 1–2 Cape buffalo depending on hunt length
  • Additional plains game opportunities
  • Professional hunter, trackers, and vehicles
  • Luxury tented camp accommodations
  • Meals, drinks, and full camp service
  • Government permits and conservation fees
  • Trophy prep, dip & pack, and export handling

What’s Not Included

  • International flights
  • Trophy fees based on animals taken
  • Travel to camp by charter or vehicle
  • Tips and personal expenses

Investment

Base safari cost ranges from $20,000–$27,000. Most clients spend $25,000–$35,000 total depending on travel and animals taken.
