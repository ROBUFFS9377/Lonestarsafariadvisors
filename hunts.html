First-Time Tanzania Buffalo Safari

7–9 Day Classic African Hunt

Experience a traditional East African buffalo safari in the Selous ecosystem, one of Tanzania’s most iconic hunting regions. This package is ideal for hunters planning their first African safari and looking for a strong mix of dangerous game and plains game.

Best For

  • First-time Africa hunters
  • Cape buffalo
  • Classic safari experience

Available Options

  • 7-Day Buffalo Safari: 1 Cape buffalo + plains game opportunities
  • 9-Day Buffalo Safari: 2 Cape buffalo + plains game opportunities

Available Species May Include

  • Cape buffalo
  • Hartebeest
  • Impala
  • Warthog
  • Wildebeest
  • Zebra

Base Safari Cost

7-Day: $20,000 1:1 / $18,000 2:1
9-Day: $27,000 1:1 / $24,000 2:1

Most clients should expect a higher all-in total once trophy fees, travel to camp, tips, and personal expenses are included.
