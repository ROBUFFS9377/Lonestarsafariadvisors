About Lone Star Safari Advisors

Lone Star Safari Advisors helps U.S. hunters plan and book authentic, high-quality Tanzania safaris with trusted outfitters.

Our Story

I’m Dillon, a Texas Army veteran with a passion for the outdoors and hunting. After seeing how confusing and risky it can be to book a safari overseas, I started Lone Star Safari Advisors to give hunters a clear, honest way to plan their first trip to Africa.

Our Mission

There’s a big gap between what hunters expect and what they actually get when booking a safari. Our goal is to close that gap—by connecting clients with proven outfitters and making sure every detail is handled the right way.

What We Do

I don’t just send you a list of outfitters. I help you:

  • Choose the right hunt based on your goals and budget
  • Avoid overpriced or poorly run operations
  • Understand the full cost before you commit
  • Navigate travel, logistics, and planning
  • Stay supported from booking through your return home

A Focus on Quality

We work with a small number of vetted outfitters in Tanzania rather than dozens of operations. This allows us to focus on quality, consistency, and making sure each client is matched with the right experience.

Built on Trust

As a veteran-owned business, Lone Star Safari Advisors is built on accountability, attention to detail, and doing things the right way—principles that carry through every hunt we help plan.

Who This Is For

  • Hunters serious about doing Africa the right way
  • Those looking for a guided, stress-free planning process
  • Clients who value quality and honesty over cutting corners
