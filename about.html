About Us

Lone Star Safari Advisors

Helping U.S. hunters plan and book authentic Tanzania safaris with clarity, confidence, and trusted guidance.

Lone Star Safari Advisors helps U.S. hunters plan and book authentic, high-quality Tanzania safaris with trusted outfitters.

Our Story

I’m Dillon, a Texas Army veteran with a passion for the outdoors and hunting. After seeing how confusing and risky it can be to book a safari overseas, I started Lone Star Safari Advisors to give hunters a clear, honest way to plan their first trip to Africa.

Our Mission

There’s a gap between what hunters expect and what they actually get when booking a safari. Our goal is to close that gap by connecting clients with proven outfitters and making sure every detail is handled the right way.

What We Do

  • Match you with the right hunt based on your goals and budget
  • Help you avoid overpriced or poorly run operations
  • Break down the full cost before you commit
  • Guide travel, logistics, and preparation
  • Stay involved from booking through your return home

Built on Trust

As a veteran-owned business, Lone Star Safari Advisors is built on accountability, attention to detail, and doing things the right way.

Selective Partnerships

We work with a small number of vetted outfitters to ensure consistent quality.

Clear Guidance

Full Support
